Travel to scenic Transylvania!

In the very beautiful land that is home to such wonderful creatures as vampires, werewolves, swamp-monsters, and politicians, you will find all sorts of natural wonders to explore!


Among the wonders you will find:

  • Snowy plains
  • Austere mountains
  • Terrifying caves filled with scary creatures
  • Charming, brainwashed country-folk

There are many attractions that can be arranged with friendly, local tour guides who are only all too happy to show you around this gorgeous land. There are even private tours of Count Dracula's castle available for advanced booking after a light screening process including blood type and career inquiry.

Food Tourism Opportunities

Whether you're a connoisseur of fine dining or you have a more specialized diet, there are many eateries and bistros that are best kept secrets among the worlds cullinary adventures. Here are just a few:

  • Wolfyburger (the fast-food chain exclusively in Transylvania)
  • Nosforatu's Fancy Italian Restaurant (they only serve up the fanciest of Italians for the most discerning of diners)
  • Papa Mummy's Nile Cuisine
  • Frankenstein's Tavern (specialty drinks and unique appetizers that could bring you back from the dead)
